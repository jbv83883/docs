## Uninstalling Vanguard

Vanguard is the anti-cheat software used by Riot Games for Valorant. Follow the steps below to uninstall it:

### Step 1: Open the Control Panel

1. **Open the Start Menu**
    - Click the `Start` button or press the `Windows` key on your keyboard.

2. **Search for Control Panel**
    - Type `Control Panel` in the search bar and select the `Control Panel` app from the search results.

### Step 2: Uninstall Vanguard

1. **Navigate to Programs and Features**
    - In the Control Panel, click on `Programs` and then select `Programs and Features`.

2. **Find Vanguard**
    - In the list of installed programs, look for `Riot Vanguard`.

3. **Uninstall Vanguard**
    - Right-click on `Riot Vanguard` and select `Uninstall`.
    - Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the uninstallation.

<Note>
You might need to restart your computer to complete the removal process.
</Note>

### Step 3: Confirm Uninstallation

1. **Restart Your Computer**
    - Restart your computer to ensure all components of Vanguard are removed.

2. **Check for Residual Files**
    - After restarting, check for any remaining Vanguard files in the installation directory (usually `C:\Program Files\Riot Vanguard`). Delete any remaining files manually.

## Uninstalling FaceIT

FaceIT provides a competitive gaming platform with its own anti-cheat software. Follow the steps below to uninstall it:

### Step 1: Open the Control Panel

1. **Open the Start Menu**
    - Click the `Start` button or press the `Windows` key on your keyboard.

2. **Search for Control Panel**
    - Type `Control Panel` in the search bar and select the `Control Panel` app from the search results.

### Step 2: Uninstall FaceIT

1. **Navigate to Programs and Features**
    - In the Control Panel, click on `Programs` and then select `Programs and Features`.

2. **Find FaceIT**
    - In the list of installed programs, look for `FACEIT` and `FACEIT Anti-Cheat`.

3. **Uninstall FaceIT**
    - Right-click on `FACEIT` and select `Uninstall`. Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the uninstallation.
    - Repeat the process for `FACEIT Anti-Cheat`.

<Note>
You might need to restart your computer to complete the removal process.
</Note>

### Step 3: Confirm Uninstallation

1. **Restart Your Computer**
    - Restart your computer to ensure all components of FaceIT are removed.

2. **Check for Residual Files**
    - After restarting, check for any remaining FaceIT files in the installation directory (usually `C:\Program Files\FACEIT`). Delete any remaining files manually.
