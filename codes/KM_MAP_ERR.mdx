title: 'KM_MAP_ERR'
description: 'Unable to map the SYS via kernel [KM_MAP_ERR]'

---

<Steps>

  <Step title="Disable Antivirus Protection">

    - Click Windows Search and search `"Virus & Threat Protection"` open the application, and click `"Manage settings"` Disable each option shown this includes, `Real-time protection`, `Cloud-delivered protection`, `Automatic sample submission`, `Tamper protection`

  </Step>
</Steps>
