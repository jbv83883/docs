## Unlinking Accounts

### Steam

1. **Logout from Steam**
    - Open Steam and log out of your current account.
    - Alternatively, open Task Manager, search for "Steam", right-click, and select "End Task".

2. **Disable Steam Startup (Optional)**
    - Open Task Manager, go to the `Startup` tab.
    - Right-click on Steam and click `Disable`.

### Discord

1. **Deauthorize Discord**
    - Open Discord and go to `Settings` > `Authorized Apps`.
    - Search for "FiveM" and select `Deauthorize`.

<Warning>Do not reauthorize your Discord when FiveM asks you to. If needed, create an alt account and authorize it.</Warning>

### Cfx.re

1. **Deauthorize Cfx.re**
    - Log in to the CFX forums with your main CFX account.
    - Go to `Settings` > `Preferences` > `Apps` and click the `Deauthorize` button.

### Remove Email & Accounts

1. **Remove Accounts**
    - Go to `Settings` > `Accounts` > `Email & accounts`.
    - Remove any email addresses listed. You must switch to a Local account, then remove your emails & accounts.
