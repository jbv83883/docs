## Unlinking Accounts & Traces
<Steps>
  <Step title="Steam">
    Open Steam, click your profile in the top right, then click change account. Then close steam, this will make sure you are logged out of your banned account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discord">
    Open Discord, go to your settings and click on `Authorized Apps` search for "FiveM" and click deauthorise.
    <Warning>
      FiveM Will ask to reauthorise Discord when you load FiveM. Do not authorise Discord on a banned account.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="CFX.re">
    <Info>This step is only if you have a CFX account connected.</Info>
    Head to https://forum.cfx.re/ login to your banned account, go to settings > Security and Deauthorise any devices connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Windows Local Account">
    Go to your Windows settings > Accounts > Your info and switch to local account, this may vary due to Windows 10 / 11. It's best you use a youtube guide if you are confused.
    
    Windows 10: https://youtu.be/cdY1wVpwoVM<br/>
    Windows 11: https://youtu.be/9ElHDJYzFMs
  </Step>
</Steps>
  <Note>
    Your accounts will be banned every time you load FiveM and are displayed with a FiveM ban message. If You get rebanned, or fail to unban you will need new accounts again.
  </Note>

## Remove Email & Accounts
  <Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate Email & Accounts">
    Open Windows settings > `Accounts` > `Email & Accounts`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove Connected Emails">
    For each email address you see, remove the email. Once completed restart your device.
      <Info>
    You must be on a local account before you can remove emails.
  </Info>
  </Step>
</Steps>
