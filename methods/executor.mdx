### Injecting to FiveM
<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to SuperLoader">
    Open SuperLoader as administrator, enter your license then simply login. Once logged in open FiveM Executor page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to FiveM">
    Open FiveM and connect to a server, wait until you are in the servers loading screen (not downloading resources) then inject via the Loader.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Load Custom .LUA Files
<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Custom Menus">
    Open File Explorer, and go to `C:\Menus` (the loader will automatically create this) then place all your .lua files here.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load .lua">
    click load .lua on Executor then all the lua menus you installed will be shown in a list, click the one you want and then inject.
  </Step>
</Steps>
